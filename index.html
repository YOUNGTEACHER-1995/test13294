What is Color Accessibility?

Color accessibility ensures that people with visual impairments can perceive and interact with your content. This includes color blindness and low vision.

Understanding Contrast Ratio

WCAG 2.1 requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text (AA) and 7:1 for AAA compliance. This helps users with low contrast sensitivity.
